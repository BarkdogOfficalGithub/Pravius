Pravius - Shortify your link with a click!

Pravius is a simple, anonymous and reliable URL-shortener which makes your life much easier. Thanks to use of a simple but effective secret-based solution, users can easily manage their links without the need to register for an account or disclose your private information. And all of that through the use of secure browsing with HTTPS!

We've teamed up with the hugely popular screen capture tool ScreenShottr to create a perfect mix between easy screen capture sharing and shortened URLs. Next to that, we aim to be transparent and open. Hence, we share our entire code base on our Github page. This allows anyone to collaborate with us to make our services better.

If you have a tip, a bug or a suggestion, don't hesitate to Contact Us.
